Help Center

📦 How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also check your order status in the "My Orders" section.

🔁 What is your return policy?

You can return items within 7 days of delivery. The product must be unused and in original packaging. Visit the Returns page to initiate a return.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery.

Please contact us at support@nrshoes.com or use our contact form.
